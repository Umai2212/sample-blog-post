Exploring Zion’s Hidden Trails

By Drakshayani | August 8, 2025

Introduction

Zion National Park is a hiker’s paradise, with its towering red rock canyons and breathtaking views. While popular trails like Angels Landing get all the attention, Zion’s hidden trails offer solitude and stunning scenery. This guide uncovers lesser-known paths perfect for adventurers seeking a quieter experience.

Why Choose Hidden Trails?

Hidden trails provide a chance to escape crowds and connect with nature. These paths often lead to secluded vistas, unique rock formations, and peaceful streams, making them ideal for reflective hikes or intimate group outings.

  • Less Crowded: Enjoy nature without the hustle.
  • Unique Views: Discover sights not seen on main trails.
  • Beginner-Friendly: Many hidden trails are less strenuous.

Top Hidden Trails

Here are three trails we’ve researched for their beauty and accessibility:

  • Chinle Trail: A 16-mile round trip through desert landscapes, perfect for a full-day hike.
  • Northgate Peaks: A moderate 4.4-mile trail with panoramic views of Zion’s east side.
  • Canyon Overlook: A short 1-mile hike with a stunning viewpoint, great for beginners.
